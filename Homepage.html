Are you struggling to stay motivated in math? Are the classes too boring for you? If so, this game is just for you!

This Fantasy Math League is similar to Fantasy Football, with a couple of changes.

Players will have teams consisting of current AP Calculus BC students. There will be a matchup between players every time there is a test.

The points are tallied by adding test scores of all STARTING mathletes. The winner receives a W and the loser takes the L. Playoffs begin during finals.

The big twist is that out of the 5 starting players, one of them must be yourself! This adds incentive to study harder!!!

Good luck to all players. Updates will be posted on this site.
